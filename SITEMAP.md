# StartupAI — Core Sitemap

## 1. Public Website (Core)
*   `/` — Landing Page (Editorial Hero & Value Prop)
*   `/features` — Capabilities & Ecosystem Detail
*   `/how-it-works` — The Strategic Methodology
*   `/pricing` — Membership & Equity-based Tiers
*   `/login` — Founder Access
*   `/signup` — Onboarding & Intake

## 2. App Shell (3-Panel OS)
All internal routes share the 3-panel architecture: **Left (Context) | Main (Work) | Right (Intelligence)**.

### A. Command Center
*   `/dashboard` — Readiness scores, critical path, next actions.

### B. Foundation (Source of Truth)
*   `/startup-profile` — Identity, mission, and proof.
*   `/lean-canvas` — 9-block business model strategy.
*   `/market-research` — AI-powered competitive benchmarks & TAM/SAM analysis.

### C. Planning (Build the Product)
*   `/roadmap` — Visual milestones (Now/Next/Later).
*   `/mvp-scope` — Feature prioritization & ruthlessness.
*   `/prd` — Technical requirement documentation.
*   `/tech-stack` — Architecture & infrastructure mapping.

### D. Finance & Fundraising
*   `/financials` — Burn, runway, and unit economics.
*   `/pitch-decks` — Narrative-first slide management.
    *   `/pitch-decks/:deck_id` — Editor view.
    *   `/pitch-decks/:deck_id/share` — Viewer link.

### E. Execution System
*   `/tasks` — High-priority tactical actions.
*   `/documents` — Repository of core startup IP.
*   `/chat` — Internal strategic messaging.

### F. Governance
*   `/agents` — Interactive strategic co-founders.
*   `/settings` — Workspace & system configurations.

## 3. MVP Founder Flow (Navigation Order)
1.  **Profile** (Who are we?)
2.  **Canvas** (How do we win?)
3.  **Market Research** (Is anyone else doing this?)
4.  **Roadmap** (When do we build?)
5.  **MVP Scope** (What is the minimum?)
6.  **PRD** (How does it work?)
7.  **Tech Stack** (What tools do we use?)
8.  **Financials** (How long do we have?)
9.  **Pitch Deck** (How do we raise?)
10. **Execution** (Do the work.)
